Jewel Match 3 Legends

About The Game

Jewel Match 3 Legends invites you on a dazzling puzzle journey through ancient lands filled with legendary treasures and magical secrets. With every successful match, you'll break curses, unlock enchanted gates, and awaken dormant magic. It's not just a game — it's a legend waiting to unfold!

FAQ

  • Is the game free to play?

    Yes, all core features are free. Optional purchases are available for boosters and lives.

  • How many levels are there?

    The game includes over 500 levels, with new ones added regularly.

  • Is an internet connection required?

    You can play offline, though events and updates require a connection.

  • Can I replay old levels?

    Absolutely! You can revisit completed stages for better scores or fun.
